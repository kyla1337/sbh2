Therapy is an essential investment in your mental and emotional well-being, and we understand that the cost can sometimes be a barrier to getting the help you need. That’s why we accept a variety of insurance plans to help ease the financial burden of therapy sessions. By using your insurance coverage, you can access professional support without the stress of high out-of-pocket expenses.

Whether you’re seeking individual counseling, couples therapy, or specialized mental health treatment, insurance can make consistent care more affordable and accessible. Our team is happy to assist you in verifying your benefits and understanding your coverage, so you can focus on your healing journey with peace of mind.
